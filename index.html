Acil Sağlık Mobile App

Need healthcare? No problem! Acil Sağlık is now with you in your smart phone…

Acil Sağlık shows the locations of open pharmacies, drugstores and hospitals on the map while drawing the closest path . Acil Sağlık provides the easiest and fastest access to health related services when you're in need.

- Drugstores

The wide network of drugstore web is integrated to Acil Sağlık app so whenever you search for a drugstore you can easily find the specific pharmacy you have need. You can access whole list of open pharmacies and also access the path that carries you to the searched medical facilites.

- Hospitals

All of the hospital information that you need comes within the Acil Sağlık app. If you want, you can create a path that involves a hospital nearby.

- Reminder

Remembering the time of drugs can be a mess when you or your relative is using more than one or two drugs at a time. Each drug requires a different specific time and that can vary among similar drugs. With Acil Sağlık you can add or delete a drug and set a reminder for the drug you have selected from the complete list of drugs. Alarm will warn you when you need to take the drug.

- Contact Us

Share your thoughts about the app, like and follow app's social accounts. Thank you for choosing our app.


*** Acil Sağlık app has won the best application award at Pamukkale Technopark Hackerspace Hackathon in May 2015.

Phrygian Way App

This app will guide you on the Phrygian Way, with maps, points of interest, accommodation places and many photos of the Phrygian Way.

Features:


- GPS navigation for the Phrygian Way routes, which works without internet access;
- Current location together with the remaining distance to your destination;
- Points of interest are listed according to their distance and grouped by type (eg accommodation, natural features). Description and several photos for each point;
- Plan your walks by choosing departure and arrival points and the app will guide you through your trip. Download only the maps you need for your chosen route.
- To focus in fine detail on your current location, (ie. while you are using the trail) switch on the‘follow me’ option from the settings.

Install now on GooglePlay
